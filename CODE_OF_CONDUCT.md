# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want PageLens to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of background or identity.

## Our standards

Be respectful and constructive. Not acceptable: harassment, personal attacks,
discriminatory or demeaning comments, or sharing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **support@iamjarl.com**. Maintainers may edit,
remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate this code, and may
ban repeat or serious offenders.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
